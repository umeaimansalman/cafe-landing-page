Our Story

A passion poured into every cup

Regard Café was built around a simple idea: exceptional coffee and genuine hospitality belong together. We serve handcrafted drinks, artisan pastries, and carefully curated flavors in a warm, inviting space designed for conversation, comfort, and connection.

At Regard Café, quality is found in the details. Thoughtfully crafted beverages, artisan pastries, and warm hospitality come together to create an experience that's inviting, memorable, and made to be enjoyed at your own pace.
